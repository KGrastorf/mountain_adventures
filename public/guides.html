Meet our Guides

Hiking Guides

Hilde

Hilde James was born with hiking boots on. She was raised in the Swiss Alps and would think nothing of a 30 mile hike across the mountain to visit a friend. She has been in the Yellowstone area for more than 20 years.

Gerald

Gerald Parkweiser was born and raised in California. Yosemite was his backyard growing up. Our expert on Yosemite, he has been with us now for 17 years.

Bruce

Bruce Hartley has been with Mountain Adventures the longest. For 28 years he has been leading the way through Glacier National Park.

Gwyneth

Gwyneth Harpsanger has ties to the Rockefellers who were so instrumental in makeing the Grand Tetons a National Park. Seems fitting that she should guide our customers around there.

Biking Guides

James

James Moribund is a classic Yellowstone cyclist. First one to ride in the spring when they open the road from West Yellowstone, he has lots of know how and stories from trips past.

Cameron

Cameron Calista from California is coming to convert you to a cyclist. This guides enthusiasm is contagious and you will enjoy the company so much you won't even realize how hard your working. Yosemite is Cameron's playgound.

Elizabeth

Elizabeth Oolong (like the tea) loves the Grand Tetons. Originally from Jackson, WY, she can tell you the eveything you might want to know from best vantage point to photograph the Teton to the stories of every lodge in the Park. You are in good hands with Elizabeth.

Harper

Harper Yesmin came out to Glacier 18 years ago and never left. Her bicycle with her since the first summer out there, she has made many trips across the Park. She can tell from just looking outside in the morning how the day is going to go. Trust Harper, she knows.

Ski Guides

Ken

Ken Fundabeen has been known to ski from Old Faithful, over to West Thumb, around the rim of the Canyon and back in a day. He is a skiing monster and loves what he does. Go out and see Yellowstone like you've never seen it before. You'll be glad you did.

Jennifer

Jennifer Smyth is a native Californian with a zest for life and the outdoors. Raised in Yosemite by her Park Ranger parents, she knows every square inch of the Park and all the stories, too.

Heidi

Heidi Schoenberg is happy to be based in the Grand Tetons. A former Park Ranger, she started with ski patrols in the winter 2 years ago and now it's all she wants to do. Let Heidi show you around some of the most picuresque scenery in the Park system.

Jorgen

Jorgen Olsen was born and raised in northern Sweden. No stranger to winters or snow, he is truly in his element when he is on his skis. You will experience Glacier National Park like never before.
